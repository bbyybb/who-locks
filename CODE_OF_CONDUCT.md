# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct / 贡献者行为准则

## Our Pledge / 我们的承诺

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

我们作为社区的成员、贡献者和领导者，承诺让每个人的参与都不受骚扰，无论年龄、体型、残障、种族、性别特征、性别认同与表达、经验水平、教育背景、社会经济地位、国籍、外貌、宗教或性取向。

## Our Standards / 我们的标准

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment / 有助于积极环境的行为：

* Using welcoming and inclusive language / 使用友善和包容的语言
* Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences / 尊重不同的观点和经验
* Gracefully accepting constructive criticism / 优雅地接受建设性批评
* Focusing on what is best for the community / 关注对社区最有利的事情
* Showing empathy towards other community members / 对社区成员表示同理心

Examples of unacceptable behavior / 不可接受的行为：

* The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention / 使用性暗示语言或图像
* Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks / 挑衅、侮辱或贬损评论
* Public or private harassment / 公开或私下骚扰
* Publishing others' private information without explicit permission / 未经许可公开他人私人信息
* Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate / 其他合理认为不当的行为

## Enforcement / 执行

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported by opening an issue or contacting the project maintainer.
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

如有违反行为准则的情况，请通过 Issue 或直接联系项目维护者举报。所有投诉将被及时公正地审查和处理。
